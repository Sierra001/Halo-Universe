Novels & Expanded Lore

Explore the rich literary universe beyond the games

The Expanded Halo Universe

The Halo franchise extends far beyond the games, with over 30 novels, comics, and short stories that explore every corner of the universe. From the origins of the Spartan program to the political machinations of the Covenant, these works provide depth and context to the epic saga.

The Fall of Reach

Eric Nylund 2001

The novel that started it all, detailing the origins of the Spartan program and Master Chief's early career. Released as a tie-in to Halo: Combat Evolved.

  • Spartan origins story
  • Prequel to Halo: CE
  • Essential reading for fans
  • 352 pages
Learn More

The Flood

William C. Dietz 2003

A novelization of the first game, expanding on the events of Halo: Combat Evolved with additional perspectives and story elements not shown in the game.

  • Game adaptation
  • First Contact with Flood
  • Additional storylines
  • 416 pages
Learn More

First Strike

Eric Nylund 2003

Bridges the gap between Halo: Combat Evolved and Halo 2, following Master Chief's return to Earth and the beginning of the Covenant's invasion.

  • Bridge novel between Halo CE and 2
  • Earth invasion setup
  • Character development
  • 400 pages
Learn More

Ghosts of Onyx

Eric Nylund 2006

Introduces the Spartan-III program and the mysterious Forerunner planet Onyx, setting up future storylines in the expanded universe.

  • Spartan-III program
  • Forerunner mysteries
  • Expanded universe setup
  • 384 pages

Cryptum

The first book in the Forerunner Saga, exploring the ancient history of the Forerunner civilization and their war with the Flood.

  • Forerunner history

Shadows of Reach

Set after Halo 5, following Master Chief's return to Reach to recover crucial assets from CASTLE Base. Connects to Halo Infinite.

Recommended Reading Order

For new readers, here's a recommended reading order to best experience the Halo expanded universe chronologically:

  1. Halo: Cryptum (Forerunner Saga #1) - 100,000+ years before Halo CE
  2. Halo: Primordium (Forerunner Saga #2) - Continues Forerunner story
  3. Halo: Silentium (Forerunner Saga #3) - Conclusion of Forerunner Saga
  4. Halo: Contact Harvest - First contact with Covenant (2524)
  5. Halo: The Fall of Reach - Spartan origins (2525-2552)
  6. Halo: The Flood - Halo: CE events (2552)
  7. Halo: First Strike - Between Halo CE and Halo 2 (2552)
  8. Halo: Ghosts of Onyx - Spartan-III program (2552)
  9. Halo: Glasslands (Kilo-Five Trilogy #1) - Post-war (2553)
  10. Halo: The Thursday War (Kilo-Five Trilogy #2) - Continues post-war story
  11. Halo: Mortal Dictata (Kilo-Five Trilogy #3) - Conclusion of Kilo-Five
  12. Halo: Shadow of Intent - Post-war Sangheili story
  13. Halo: Envoy - Jiralhanae conflict
  14. Halo: Retribution - Grey Team story
  15. Halo: Silent Storm - Early Master Chief missions
  16. Halo: Oblivion - Sequel to Silent Storm
  17. Halo: Shadows of Reach - Post-Halo 5, pre-Infinite
  18. Halo: Divine Wind - Latest novel in timeline
