Halo Wars 2

Lead the Firefight

Game Information

Release Date: February 21, 2017
Developer: Creative Assembly, 343 Industries
Platform: Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 79/100

Synopsis

Halo Wars 2 is a real-time strategy video game developed by Creative Assembly and 343 Industries, and published by Microsoft Studios. Released on February 21, 2017, it is the sequel to Halo Wars and continues the story of the UNSC Spirit of Fire crew 28 years after the events of the first game.

The story follows Captain James Cutter, Professor Ellen Anders, and the AI Isabel as they awaken from cryo-sleep near the Ark, a massive Forerunner installation. They discover the Banished, a faction of mercenaries led by the Brute warlord Atriox, who has taken control of the Ark. The crew must rally UNSC forces to prevent the Banished from using the Ark's power to unleash devastation across the galaxy.

Key Features

  • Sequel to Halo Wars set 28 years later
  • Introduces the Banished faction from Halo Infinite
  • Blitz mode - card-based real-time strategy gameplay
  • Enhanced graphics and performance
  • Cross-play between Xbox One and Windows 10
  • New leaders with unique abilities
  • Expanded multiplayer options
  • Post-launch DLC campaigns
