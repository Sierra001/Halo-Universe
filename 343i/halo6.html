Halo Infinite

The Next Step in Halo Evolution

Game Information

Release Date: December 8, 2021
Developer: 343 Industries
Platform: Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Windows
Metacritic Score: 87/100

Synopsis

Halo Infinite is a first-person shooter developed by 343 Industries and published by Xbox Game Studios. Released on December 8, 2021, it is the sixth mainline entry in the Halo series and the third game in the "Reclaimer Saga" following Halo 5: Guardians. The game serves as a spiritual reboot for the franchise, returning to the series' roots while introducing new gameplay innovations.

Set in 2560, the story follows the Master Chief as he battles the Banished, a faction of mercenaries that have taken control of the Zeta Halo ring. After being defeated in space, Chief must fight his way across the ring to rescue captured UNSC personnel and uncover the secrets of a mysterious entity known as "The Weapon."

Key Features

  • Open-world "Halo Ring" environment to explore
  • Free-to-play multiplayer with seasonal content
  • Grappleshot equipment for enhanced mobility
  • New enemy faction: The Banished
  • Equipment system with various tactical options
  • Advanced AI companions with distinct personalities
  • Cross-platform play and progression
  • Campaign co-op and mission replay
