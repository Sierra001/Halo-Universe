Halo 4

A New Saga Begins

Game Information

Release Date: November 6, 2012
Developer: 343 Industries
Platform: Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 87/100

Synopsis

Halo 4 is a first-person shooter video game developed by 343 Industries and published by Microsoft Studios for the Xbox 360. The game was released worldwide in November 2012. Halo 4 is the seventh title in the Halo franchise and the first game in the Reclaimer Saga, returning the story focus to the Master Chief and his AI companion Cortana.

The story follows Master Chief as he awakens from cryo-sleep aboard the wreckage of the UNSC Forward Unto Dawn, adrift in space near a mysterious Forerunner planet known as Requiem. Chief must combat both the Covenant remnants and a new enemy, the ancient Forerunner warriors known as the Prometheans, while trying to save Cortana from rampant deterioration.

Key Features

  • First mainline Halo game developed by 343 Industries
  • Introduction of the Prometheans - a new enemy faction
  • Enhanced graphics and audio design
  • Deeper character development focusing on Master Chief and Cortana
  • Revamped multiplayer with Spartan Ops cooperative episodic content
  • New weapons, vehicles, and gameplay mechanics
  • Integration with Halo Waypoint for stats tracking
