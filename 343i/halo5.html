Halo 5: Guardians

Hunt the Truth

Game Information

Release Date: October 27, 2015
Developer: 343 Industries
Platform: Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 84/100

Synopsis

Halo 5: Guardians is a first-person shooter developed by 343 Industries and published by Microsoft Studios for the Xbox One. Released worldwide in October 2015, it is the tenth installment in the Halo series and the second in the "Reclaimer Saga" following Halo 4. The game continues the story of Master Chief and introduces a new team of Spartan-IVs called Fireteam Osiris.

The campaign follows two separate squads: Blue Team (Master Chief, Kelly-087, Fred-104, and Linda-058) and Fireteam Osiris (Jameson Locke, Holly Tanaka, Edward Buck, and Olympia Vale). The story focuses on Master Chief's apparent desertion and his search for Cortana, who has returned as a powerful entity known as Cortana.

Key Features

  • First Halo game designed exclusively for Xbox One with 60fps gameplay
  • Squad-based gameplay with AI teammates and command system
  • Advanced mobility system with thrusters, ground pounds, and clambering
  • Warzone mode - large-scale 24-player PvPvE battles
  • REQ system for cosmetic and gameplay item acquisition
  • Enhanced Forge mode with significantly more features and objects
  • Dedicated servers for all multiplayer modes
