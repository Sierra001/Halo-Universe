Halo Wars

Command the Battlefield

Game Information

Release Date: February 26, 2009
Developer: Ensemble Studios (Published by 343 Industries)
Platform: Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 82/100

Synopsis

Halo Wars is a real-time strategy video game developed by Ensemble Studios and published by Microsoft Game Studios for the Xbox 360. Released on February 26, 2009, it is the first Halo game not developed by Bungie and the first in the series to be a real-time strategy game rather than a first-person shooter.

Set in 2531, 21 years before the events of Halo: Combat Evolved, the game follows the crew of the UNSC Spirit of Fire as they battle the Covenant on the colony world of Harvest and later the Forerunner planet known as the Shield World. The story focuses on Sergeant John Forge, Professor Ellen Anders, and the AI Serena as they uncover ancient Forerunner secrets.

Key Features

  • Real-time strategy gameplay optimized for console controllers
  • Command UNSC forces against the Covenant
  • Base building and resource management
  • Hero units with special abilities
  • Cooperative campaign mode
  • Multiplayer with various game modes
  • Leader powers for strategic advantages
  • Prequel story expanding Halo lore
