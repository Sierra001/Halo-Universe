About This Archive

A comprehensive guide to the Halo Universe

Project Mission

The Halo Universe Archive was created to provide fans and newcomers alike with a comprehensive, organized, and visually appealing resource covering the entire Halo franchise. From the original Bungie games to the current 343 Industries era, this site aims to catalog and celebrate one of gaming's most iconic science fiction universes.

About The Creator

Halo Archivist

Halo Enthusiast & Universe Chronicler

As a lifelong Halo fan since the release of Combat Evolved in 2001, I've followed the franchise through every installment, novel, and piece of expanded lore. This project represents years of passion for one of gaming's richest universes.

My goal is to create a resource that honors both Bungie's original vision and 343 Industries' continuation of the saga, while providing an accessible entry point for new fans discovering the series.
