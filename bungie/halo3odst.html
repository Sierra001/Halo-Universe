Halo 3: ODST

Prepare to Drop

Game Information

Release Date: September 22, 2009
Developer: Bungie Studios
Platform: Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 83/100

Synopsis

Halo 3: ODST (Orbital Drop Shock Troopers) is a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie Studios. Originally conceived as an expansion to Halo 3, it was released as a standalone title on September 22, 2009. The game takes place during the events of Halo 2 and follows a group of ODST soldiers as they battle the Covenant in the ruined city of New Mombasa.

Players assume the role of "The Rookie" as they explore the open-world hub of New Mombasa at night, piecing together what happened to their missing squadmates through flashback missions. The game features a darker, more atmospheric tone than previous Halo games and introduces the cooperative Firefight mode.

Key Features

  • Play as ODST soldiers instead of Spartans
  • Open-world investigative hub in New Mombasa
  • Firefight mode - cooperative survival against waves of enemies
  • Darker, more atmospheric storytelling
  • New health system (no energy shields)
  • VISR (Visual Intelligence System, Recon) mode for night vision
  • All 24 Halo 3 multiplayer maps included

Campaign Missions

  1. Prepare to Drop (Prologue)
  2. Mombasa Streets (Hub)
  3. Tayari Plaza (Flashback)
  4. Uplift Reserve (Flashback)

  6. ONI Alpha Site (Flashback)
  7. NMPD HQ (Flashback)
  8. Kikowani Station (Flashback)
  9. Data Hive (Flashback)
  10. Coastal Highway (Finale)
