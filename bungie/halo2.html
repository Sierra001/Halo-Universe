Halo 2

The Battle for Earth Has Begun

Game Information

Release Date: November 9, 2004
Developer: Bungie Studios
Platform: Xbox, Windows, Xbox 360, Xbox One
Metacritic Score: 95/100

Synopsis

Halo 2 is a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie Studios and published by Microsoft Game Studios. Released on November 9, 2004, the game is the second installment in the Halo franchise and continues the story of the Master Chief as he defends Earth from the Covenant while also exploring the perspectives of the alien alliance.

The game introduced dual-wielding weapons, vehicular combat improvements, and online multiplayer via Xbox Live. Halo 2's narrative is notable for its split perspective, allowing players to control both the Master Chief and the Covenant Elite known as the Arbiter, providing insight into the Covenant's internal politics and religious motivations.

Key Features

  • Dual-wielding weapons for the first time in the series
  • Online multiplayer via Xbox Live with matchmaking and parties
  • Dual narrative perspective (Master Chief and the Arbiter)
  • Improved graphics and expanded game engine capabilities
  • Vehicle boarding and hijacking mechanics
  • Expanded Covenant lore and character development
  • Cliffhanger ending that directly led to Halo 3
