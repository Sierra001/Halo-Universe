Halo: Reach

From the Beginning, You Know the End

Game Information

Release Date: September 14, 2010
Developer: Bungie Studios
Platform: Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 91/100

Synopsis

Halo: Reach is a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie Studios and published by Microsoft Game Studios for the Xbox 360. Released on September 14, 2010, it is the sixth installment in the Halo series and serves as a direct prequel to Halo: Combat Evolved, chronicling the Fall of Reach, humanity's last major stronghold before Earth.

The game follows Noble Team, a squad of Spartan-III supersoldiers, during the Covenant invasion of the planet Reach. As Bungie's final Halo game before handing the franchise over to 343 Industries, Halo: Reach was both a critical and commercial success, praised for its emotional storytelling, improved gameplay mechanics, and robust multiplayer features.

Key Features

  • Prequel story to the original Halo trilogy
  • Armor Abilities system replacing equipment
  • Customizable armor with extensive options
  • Invasion multiplayer mode (Spartans vs Elites)
  • Forge World - massive map for custom creations
  • Theater mode improvements
  • Credit-based progression system
  • Firefight mode with customization options
