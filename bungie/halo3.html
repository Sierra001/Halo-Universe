Halo 3

Finish the Fight

Game Information

Release Date: September 25, 2007
Developer: Bungie Studios
Platform: Xbox 360, Xbox One, Windows
Metacritic Score: 94/100

Synopsis

Halo 3 is a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie Studios for the Xbox 360 console. Released worldwide on September 25, 2007, it is the third installment in the Halo franchise and concludes the story arc that began with Halo: Combat Evolved and continued in Halo 2.

The game continues the story of the Master Chief as he returns to Earth to finish the fight against the Covenant and the Flood. Halo 3 introduces new vehicles, weapons, and gameplay features, along with a significantly expanded suite of multiplayer and online options through Xbox Live.

Key Features

  • Concludes the original Halo trilogy's story arc
  • Four-player cooperative campaign mode
  • Theater mode for saving and sharing gameplay clips
  • Forge mode for custom map creation and editing
  • Enhanced graphics and physics engine
  • Equipment system with deployable items
  • Expanded multiplayer with 24 achievements
