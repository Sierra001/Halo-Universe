The Legendary Saga

The Halo franchise is a military science fiction media franchise created by Bungie and currently managed by 343 Industries, a subsidiary of Xbox Game Studios.

Bungie Era (2001-2010)

Starting with Halo: Combat Evolved, Bungie Studios created five mainline games that defined the Xbox platform and revolutionized first-person shooters on consoles.

343 Industries Era (2011-Present)

After Bungie's departure, 343 Industries took over the franchise, expanding the universe with new games, stories, and characters while honoring the original legacy.
